Innovative Solar Panel Applications

Expanding the use of solar panels beyond traditional energy generation, this idea envisions their integration into everyday devices, smart cities, and wearable technology. For example, solar-powered streetlights could enhance urban energy efficiency, while flexible solar panels could be used in clothing to charge portable devices.
