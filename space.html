Lunar Space Station: Portal of the Galaxies

This idea envisions a modular and self-sustaining lunar base, utilizing local materials (lunar regolith) for construction and high-efficiency solar energy systems. The station will serve as a hub for space exploration, scientific research, and future missions to Mars.
